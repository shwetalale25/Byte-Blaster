Driver Drowsiness and Distraction Detection System

Welcome to Driver Drowsiness and Distraction Detection

Now-a-days, driver drowsiness and distraction is a significant factor in fairly large number of vehicle accidents. Therefore, a technology to prevent and detect driver drowsiness and distraction is a major challenge in the field of accident-avoidance systems. This system moniters the eyes and behavioral characteristics so the driver can be alerted well in advance to avoid accidents and this is possible if eye closed for long time.
